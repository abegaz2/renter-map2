Rental Housing Repair Map

Renter generated submissions about repair work that needs to be done in their rental homes can help renters and organizers better understand the breadth of issues in their geographic areas

Renters' rights vary widely by state and the process of enforcing your repair rights is often onerous, especially for low-income and disabled tenants.

HUD links to different tenant resources in all fifty states.

The below map shows submitted surveys about repair work that tenants self-report. It gives information about the type of repair, whether it affects the health and safety of the tenants, and how the tenant has informed their landlord.
